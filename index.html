Laws of the Land

Rules & Guidelines

  • Respect all players and staff. No harassment or toxicity.
  • Use appropriate language. Keep chat friendly and clean.
  • No cheating, hacks, or exploiting mods.
  • Build responsibly and avoid griefing others' constructions.
  • Do not share or leak the modpack publicly; ask admins for access.
  • Keep PvP to agreed zones only.
  • Report any bugs or issues to staff promptly.

Explore the World

  • Build your own medieval house or fortress with our exclusive custom schematics.
  • Discover vast new biomes thanks to Biomes O’ Plenty and other mods.
  • Gather unique resources introduced by Age of Ores and Galosphere mods.
  • Face powerful mobs and dragons with Alex’s Mobs and Dragon Mounts Legacy.
  • Use advanced crafting and storage with Iron Furnaces and Storage Drawers.
  • Experience custom dungeons and treasures with Deeper and Darker mod.
  • Enjoy smooth gameplay with optimized mechanics and friendly community events.
